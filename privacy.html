Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

At WordPuzzleSolver, accessible from https://wordpuzzlesolver.github.io/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by WordPuzzleSolver and how we use it.

1. Client-Side Processing

Unlike many other web applications, WordPuzzleSolver is designed to be a "client-side" tool. This means that the letters you enter and the words generated are processed locally in your own web browser. Your search queries are not sent to our servers, nor are they stored by us.

2. Local Storage

The "Search History" feature uses your browser's Local Storage. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded to our servers. You can clear this history at any time using the "Clear" button within the application or by clearing your browser's cache.

3. Log Files

WordPuzzleSolver follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, and tracking users' movement on the website.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, WordPuzzleSolver uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

5. Third-Party Privacy Policies

WordPuzzleSolver's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites (such as GitHub Pages where this site is hosted). Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party services for more detailed information.

6. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
